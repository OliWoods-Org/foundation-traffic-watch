> Analyze online ads for trafficking indicators. Cross-reference phone/images across platforms. Survivor safety planning. Investigation dossiers for law enforcement.

## The Solution

**AdAnalyzer** — Detect coded language, burner phone patterns, hotel-room photo metadata in classified ads\n**CrossLinker** — Connect phone numbers and images across platforms to link cases\n**SurvivorSafety** — Anonymous safety planning, shelter location, legal rights\n**InvestigationAssistant** — Generate dossiers from public data for law enforcement\n**TipProcessor** — Receive and triage anonymous tips from the public

## Agents

MANDATORY: National Human Trafficking Hotline 1-888-373-7888. If someone is in immediate danger, call 911.
